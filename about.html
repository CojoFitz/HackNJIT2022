About Campr

Conor J Fitzpatrick, George A Tipton, Nicholas M Merlino, and Jerry Zheng set out with a single goal, to connect people in a world where virtual forests grew. Through trails and delibiration, a path was taken to allow people to camp together in virtual campsites of different sizes.

Throughout our long history of 24 hours serving camprs, Campr has succeeded in connecting fake users with completely fabricated interests. Campr has succeeded and excelled in its main goal of being a proof of concept.
